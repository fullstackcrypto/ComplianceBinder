InspectionBinder Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 26, 2026

InspectionBinder helps users organize inspection-related tasks and documents. This policy explains what information is handled by the application.

Information collected

The application stores account email addresses, password hashes, binder names, task details, uploaded document metadata, uploaded files, and billing status information received from the payment provider.

Uploaded documents

Users control what they upload. Do not upload sensitive medical, financial, resident, employee, or regulated records unless your deployment, policies, and provider agreements are configured for that type of data.

Payments

Payment processing is handled by Stripe. InspectionBinder stores plan and subscription status metadata, but does not store full card numbers.

Use of information

Information is used to provide binder organization, task tracking, document storage, report generation, billing, reminders, security logging, and support.

Data retention

Data is retained while the account is active unless deletion is requested or a separate agreement states otherwise. Operators should maintain their own backups for mission-critical records.

Security

The application uses password hashing, authenticated access, upload restrictions, and report escaping. No system can guarantee absolute security.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact the operator of your InspectionBinder deployment.
